How to play

Default keybinds are Left mouse and Right mouse buttons, and Z and X. You can modify both in the settings. Hit circles appear as the song plays. Click on the next hit circle when the approach circle closes in on it. For sliders, press and hold and move along the slider at its predetermined speed. Spinners are simple. Just press and hold and spin your mouse around the center as fast as you can.

There is a lot of input lag or delay

Try enabling "Raw input" in the settings. However, some operating systems and browsers have their own quirks that may add more input delay.

Gameplay is too laggy

Unchecking "Use system resolution" in the settings and choosing a smaller resolution than your system scaling may help with your FPS (Computers with high resolution screens may have scaling over 100%). To avoid high CPU usage, close out any other running programs and browser tabs. A restart may help, too. Make sure to use Chrome or Firefox. If you're still having problems, submit an Êèêissue.
